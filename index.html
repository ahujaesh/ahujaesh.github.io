Our Mission

We are the Daring Dragons, a Destination Imagination (DI) team dedicated to creativity and creation. We have competed in the 2021 - 2022 season, the 2022 - 2023 season, and the 2023 - 2024 DI season. Our mission is to excel in creative problem-solving and innovation.

States (TX):

We also won 1st place in the Destination Imagination States competition in the 2023 - 2024 season, advanced to Globals, and got the "Davinci" special award along with the "High IC" special award. (Which is the most awards you can get in one competition, which is really difficult. Also, getting special awards at State and Globals is much harder than at Region.)

We also won 2nd place in the Destination Imagination State competition in the 2022 - 2023 season and advanced to the Kansas City Global Finals.

Regionals (TXSE6):

This year, we won 3rd place in the Destination Imagination Regional competition in the 2023 - 2024 season, advanced to State, and got the "High IC" special award.

We also won 1st place in the Destination Imagination Regional competition in the 2022 - 2023 season, advanced to State, and got the "Davinci" special award.

We also won 2nd place in the Destination Imagination Regional competition in the 2021 - 2022 season and got the "High IC" and "Spirit of DI" special awards.
