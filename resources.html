Instant Challenges

What are instant challenges?

Destination Imagination (DI) Instant Challenges are dynamic and creative problem-solving activities that challenge participants to think quickly on their feet. These challenges are an integral part of the broader DI educational program, fostering teamwork, critical thinking, and innovation. Participants, often organized into teams, receive a prompt and have a limited time to collaboratively devise a solution. Instant Challenges cover a diverse range of scenarios, from building structures with limited materials to performing improvisational skits. The emphasis is not just on finding solutions but on doing so under time constraints, promoting adaptability and resourcefulness. These challenges provide a unique platform for participants to showcase their creative prowess, teamwork, and ability to navigate unforeseen obstacles, making them a cornerstone of the experiential learning journey within Destination Imagination. Here, we have provided a set of instant challenges for you/your team to practice with. You can read more about the Instant Challenge here.
